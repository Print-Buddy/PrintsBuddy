Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Print$Buddy, we aim to provide a seamless printing experience. This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the process for cancelling orders and requesting refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

  • Before Printing: You may cancel your order before printing begins by contacting us via WhatsApp (9251028070) or email (support@printbuddy.in). No charges will apply.
  • After Printing: Orders cannot be cancelled once printing has started, as resources (ink, paper) are already committed.

2. Refund Eligibility
